# Major Project Proposal

## Description
A recreation of the tower defense game Plants vs Zombies. Defend your lawn from the encroaching zombies with an army of plants using strategy and resource management. They can damage and kill the zombies, produce sun to make more plants, and slow the zombies' progress. Careful though, the zombies may have their own sort of tricks to eat your brains


## Needs to have
- Grid based lawn where you can plant plants and zombies can walk on
    - Plants and Zombies must be able to target/damage each those in their respective lane (potentially with some exceptions)


- Variety of Plants to use
    - Sunflower: Produces extra sun to plant extra plants
    - Peashooter: Basic dps. shoots peas to damage zombies
    - Wallnut: Has a ton of health to stall zombies, but can't fight back
    - Potato Mine: Plant the mine, which takes a bit to arm itself. once armed, it'll explode on the next zombie that steps on it
    - Cherry Bomb: Place the bomb, which will explode in a 3x3 radius after a short windup

    - I will probably make parallels to these plants (like turrets or something), making use of the mechanics primarily

    - UI to have the ability to choose which plant you want to place, alongside you needed the required cost of sun


- Sun stuff
    - Occasionally falls throughout the level
    - Extra can be produced by sunflowers
    - Has different behaviors based on how it's produced


- Zombies function
    - Small variety of zombies to fight off
        Basic: Your basic everyday zombie
        Conehead: Wears a cone on its head. Extra defense and health
        Buckethead: Super tanky zombie with even more defense and health
   
    - Kill the player and eat their brains
    - Wave based system to spawn the zombies


- At least one complete level showcasing all different zombie types
    - Adventure mode/Level select to select the level
        - Main menu therefore must exist
    - Ability to win the level by killing all waves of zombies


## Nice to have
- Pause menu (freezes everything)
- Shovel mechanic
    - removes plants in case you change your mind or made a mistake on placement
- Lawnmowers
    - The final line of defense at the very end of the lawn. Triggers upon a zombie stepping on it and kills every zombie on the lane, but is gone permanently


- More plant types. Ex:
    - SnowPea: Like a peashooter, but also halves zombies' speed
    - Repeater: Like a peashooter, but fires 2 peas per shot, doubling damage
- More zombie types. Ex:
    - Polevaulter: Has the ability to jump over a plant once
    - Balloon: Hung by a balloon in the air, being untargetable. Only certain plants can pop the balloon to make it fall to the ground and be damaged again
- More levels
    - You can obtain plants overtime
- Endless Mode
    - Fight off an endless hoard of zombies


- Custom sprites
    - I don't intend to use the sprites from the OG game, and they'd be very simple sprites most likely
    - May have animations
- Sound effects
